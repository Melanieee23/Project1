One of the big things that happens abroad is the group chats .
It is very easy to get wrapped up in these "abroad groupchats" which are made of the american kids at your school and they post where to go out. If you follow this and go where discounts are at clubs and other things, you will be surroudning yourself by only this american group putting yourself in sort of a bubble.
